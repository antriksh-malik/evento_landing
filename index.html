Evento


Be amazed with the simplicity of planning an event by yourself, right from the comfort of your home.

No more hassle!

Have an event to plan and don't know where to start or where to go. Or are tired by bouncing from one store to another and taking reviews about a vendor just from the word of mouth. Worry no more, evento gives you the power to choose vendors within your budget with the help of verified customer reviews, right from your phone!

It's easy

Just launch your app or our website. Choose the event you intend to plan and pick from the category of services that you require in your event. All that's left for you now is to choose a vendor that suits you and book them!

Or if you have an event to plan on the 10th hour and don't have the time to handpick everything, then we give you pre-made event templates! They are just the key services required for an event with the top rated vendors availible around you. You just pick your event and everything will be booked and ready to go.

We also have something that we call successful event history. It's basically just all the elements that other people used while planning their own events clubbed together in one place so that you can refer to what someone did for their own event and replicate similar results!
